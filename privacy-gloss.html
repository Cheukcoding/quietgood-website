Gloss Daily

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Gloss Daily is built to be quiet and respectful. We collect only the minimum we need to run the app and keep your words safe across your devices. We use no trackers, no advertising, and no analytics — and we never sell your personal data. This policy explains, in plain English, what we hold and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

Automatically, when you first open the app

The app creates an anonymous account for you — a random ID — so it works instantly with no sign-up. At this stage we do not collect your name or email address.

Your content

The things you create in the app:

  • Words you save and custom words you add yourself
  • Tags and short notes you attach to words
  • Your preferences, such as reading level and reminder time

Only if you choose to sign in

Signing in with Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google is optional. If you do, we receive your email address and your name (if the provider shares it). With Sign in with Apple you can use Hide My Email, in which case we only ever see a private Apple relay address rather than your real one.

What we do NOT collect

  • Precise location
  • Contacts
  • Photos or your photo library
  • Health data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting data
  • Push notification tokens (the daily reminder is on-device only)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information above only to run the app for you:

  • To run the app and sync your words, tags, notes, and preferences
  • To restore your words across your devices when you sign in
  • To deliver the once-a-day on-device reminder, if you turn it on
  • To keep your account secure

We do not use your information for advertising or profiling.

How Your Data Is Stored & Shared

Your content is stored locally on your device and also synced to our cloud database under your account ID, so you don’t lose it.

  • Our cloud database is Cloud Firestore, and accounts are managed by Firebase Authentication (including anonymous sign-in) — both part of Google Firebase, acting as our data processor.
  • If you choose to sign in, Apple and/or Google process that sign-in to confirm your identity.

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share it for marketing. You can read the relevant third-party policies here: Apple’s Privacy Policy and Google’s Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account exists — that is, until you delete your account or ask us to delete it. See Deleting Your Account & Data below.

Your Rights & Choices

You are in control of your data. You can:

  • Access, correct, or export the words, tags, and notes you’ve created
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Turn the daily reminder off at any time
  • Sign out to return the app to a fresh anonymous state

If you are in the UK or EU, you have rights under UK GDPR (and the EU GDPR), including the rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your data. You may contact us at any time, and you also have the right to complain to a supervisory authority — in the UK, the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we hold, to request its deletion, and not to be discriminated against for exercising those rights. We do not sell personal information.

Deleting Your Account & Data

You can permanently delete your account and everything associated with it in one of two ways:

1. In the app

Go to Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data, both in the cloud and on your device.

2. By email

Email us at cheuk.coding@gmail.com and we will delete your account and data within 30 days.

Please note: signing out alone does not delete your data — it simply returns the app to a fresh anonymous state.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data, including encrypted transport over HTTPS between the app and our cloud database. No method of transmission or storage is ever 100% secure, but we work to keep your information protected.

Children’s Privacy

Gloss Daily is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

International Users

Because we use Google Firebase, your data may be processed on Google servers located outside the United Kingdom. Where data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers, as required by UK and EU data protection law.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will change the “Last updated” date above. For material changes, we will also note them inside the app.
