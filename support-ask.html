01

Getting started

A small tool for less small talk. Ask Slowly is a card deck of 300 real questions, organised into 5 decks — Couples, Friends, Family, Colleagues, and Teammates — each with three depth levels: warm-up, closer, and deep.

Pick someone you care about. Pick a depth. The question does the rest.

02

Using the app

Pick a deck → pick a depth level (warm-up, closer, or deep) → a question appears. Read it aloud, both of you answer, then shuffle for the next one. There's no scoring, no winning, no timer.

The best conversations happen when you give an answer enough room to breathe.

Level 1 — warm-up. Light, observational, easy to answer. Good for getting started or for evenings when you don't want to think too hard.

Level 2 — closer. A step in. Personal but not raw. The kind of question that opens a door.

Level 3 — deep. Tender. Slower. For people you trust and quiet rooms.
