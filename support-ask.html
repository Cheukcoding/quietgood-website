Getting started

ASK Slowly is a card deck for deeper conversations. Pick a card, ask the question, listen — that's it. The cards are designed to help couples, friends, colleagues, and teammates understand each other better, one slow question at a time.

No. There is no sign-up, no email required, and no login. Open the app and start.

Using the app

Open a deck, draw a card, and take turns answering. One question, both people speak. There is no scoring, no winning, and no rush. The best conversations happen when you give an answer enough room to breathe.

Yes. Many people use ASK Slowly as a solo reflection prompt — draw one card a day, sit with it, write your own answer down somewhere. The cards work whether you have a partner across the table or only yourself.

The decks are organised by the kind of conversation you want — gentle ice-breakers, getting-to-know-you, deeper reflections, and more. Browse the deck list in the app to find what fits the moment.

The app is free to download. If there are any optional in-app purchases — such as additional decks — they will always be clearly labelled inside the app. There are no ads and no tracking.

03

Privacy & data

None. ASK Slowly has no servers, no analytics, and no tracking SDKs. The conversations you have are your own — we never see them because we never receive them.

Read the full Privacy Notice for the longer version.

ASK Slowly is intentionally designed not to record your spoken answers. Cards are a prompt, not a transcript. If you'd like to keep an answer, write it down somewhere private — a notebook, the Notes app, anywhere you trust.

Press and hold the app icon on your home screen, then tap "Remove App." That removes ASK Slowly and any local state — no extra cleanup required, because nothing was ever sent off your device.
