1. Who we are

ASK is an independent mobile application that provides curated card decks with deep, meaningful questions — designed to help couples, friends, colleagues, and teammates understand each other better ("we," "us," or "our").

This notice explains what data we collect (very little), how it is used, and how it is protected.

Contact: cheuk.coding@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

ASK does not require an account and does not transmit any personal data to external servers. All app activity stays entirely on your device.

The only data stored is local app state (such as which cards you have viewed), kept solely on your device and never sent anywhere. This data is automatically removed when you delete the app.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs of any kind.

3. How we use your data

Because ASK collects no personal data, there is nothing to process, share, or sell. The app functions entirely offline using content bundled within the app itself. No internet connection is required to use ASK.

4. Third-party services

ASK does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud services. The only third-party involvement is Apple's standard App Store delivery infrastructure, which is governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy.

5. Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar laws, you have rights regarding your personal data. Because ASK does not collect personal data, most of these rights are satisfied by default — there is no data for us to hold, share, or delete on our end.

Access No personal data is held by us
Erasure Delete the app to remove all local state
Portability No personal data to export
Objection No processing to object to

If you have any questions about your rights, please contact us at the email below.

6. Children's privacy

ASK does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions). Since no data is collected at all, there is no risk of unintended collection from younger users.

We recommend that parents and guardians review card content with younger children, as some questions are intended for mature audiences.

7. California privacy rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with additional rights regarding your personal information.

We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information. We do not use personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising. There is no personal data to request, delete, or restrict.

If you have questions about your CCPA rights, contact us at cheuk.coding@gmail.com.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
